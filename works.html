    "Listen. Remember what you're feeling in this moment. As long as you know in your heart where you're heading, you are guaranteed to see the ending of your story."

    Stelle's heart trembled as it desperately tried to cling to what she was feeling. To make it the destination of her heart. She knew the woman meant that she needed to chase after the future this 'Elio' had foreseen but it had settled on something else. It wanted to chase after her. Like an unfulfilled promise she couldn't remember. Stelle nodded to convey that she understood, that she had chosen where her heart was heading.

    Kafka saw the look in Stelle's eyes. She had chosen what to chase after, and Kafka knew it was her, and it made her smile. 'If Elio foresaw everything, then surely this was what he wanted to happen.' She told herself over and over and over again, holding it close to her heart as she continued, "That's the you I like."
